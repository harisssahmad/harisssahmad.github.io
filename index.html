Who Are We

We are a group of enthusiasts working together to create websites and android applications. Our main focus is to create the final build of Educationist, which is currently in beta version.


What is Educationist

Educationist provides free e-books and past papers for all major CAIE Ordinary Level appearing subjects in a user-friendly way. Moreover, Educationist also promises to help you with your queries about exam guidance, study materials and even the chemistry homework you are unable to do, just ask help from any one of our "ustaads"!


What will be Educationist

Educationist will still provide e-books and past papers in a user-friendly way, but not only for O'Level subjects.

Moreover, Educationist will still help you with your queries about exam guidance, study materials and even the chemistry homework you are unable to do. You will still be able to ask help from any one of our "ustaads", but now everything will be formed in to posts and not only ustaads, but everyone will be able to interact and help others in answering queries.

And, we will also be expanding our team of ustaads.
